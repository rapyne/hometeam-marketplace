Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about using HomeTeam.

How do I find the right practitioner?

Use our search and filter tools to browse practitioners by specialty, therapeutic approach, session type, and identity. Read their profiles, check reviews from other community members, and book an initial consultation to see if it's a good fit.

Are all practitioners verified?

Yes! Every practitioner on HomeTeam undergoes a thorough verification process. We verify their professional credentials, licensure, and commitment to providing inclusive, affirming care.

How much do sessions cost?

Session prices vary by practitioner and are displayed transparently on each profile. Many practitioners offer sliding scale fees, and you can filter by price range to find options within your budget. Sessions typically range from $75 to $250.

Do you accept insurance?

Insurance acceptance varies by practitioner. Each practitioner's profile lists which insurance plans they accept. We also provide superbills that you can submit to your insurance for potential reimbursement.

How do I become a practitioner on the platform?

Click "Join as Practitioner" and complete our application. We'll review your credentials, verify your licensure, and set up your profile. The process typically takes 3-5 business days.

Can I have virtual sessions?

Absolutely! Many of our practitioners offer telehealth sessions. You can filter for virtual-only practitioners in the search. Our platform supports secure video sessions to ensure your privacy.

What if I'm in crisis?

If you're in immediate danger, please call 911. For mental health crises, contact the 988 Suicide and Crisis Lifeline by calling or texting 988. The Crisis Text Line is also available ‚Äî text HOME to 741741. HomeTeam is not a crisis service.
